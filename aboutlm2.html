ABOUT ASSEMBLY OF NATIONS

After few years of anticipation, Calcutta Boys’ School proudly announces the return of its prestigious diplomatic conclave - the Assembly of Nations (CBS AON) 2025 - on the 3rd and 4th of July, 2025. Building on the legacy of its acclaimed editions in 2011, 2013, 2015 and 2019, CBS AON re-emerges with its Fifth Edition as a beacon in Kolkata’s Model United Nations circuit, guided by its inspiring motto:

Renascentia De Legatum - The Rebirth of Legacy.

CBS AON which started its journey for the first time in the year 2011 is more than just a MUN - it is a destination for schools seeking an intellectually stimulating and transformative experience. Recognized across the city as a hallmark of quality in the Model United Nations (MUN) circuit, CBS AON stands out for its innovative committees, challenging agendas, and a seasoned team of chairpersons.

CBS AON has always been more than just a conference-it is a destination for schools seeking a truly enriching diplomatic experience. From its meticulous organization to its vibrant community of young leaders, CBS AON offers the perfect platform for delegates to sharpen their skills, broaden their perspectives, and forge lasting connections. Here, delegates do not just discuss world affairs; they shape solutions, forge friendships, and become part of a tradition that celebrates excellence, leadership, and the relentless spirit of renewal.

This year’s Assembly of Nations promises an unparalleled experience, bringing together the city’s brightest young minds for two days of rigorous debate, strategic negotiation, and the pursuit of consensus amidst the vibrant chaos of international diplomacy. Delegates can expect two days filled with spirited debate, strategic negotiation, and the unique thrill of transforming chaos into consensus, all within an environment that champions knowledge, diplomacy, and leadership.Featuring dynamic committees, engaging topics, and an experienced panel of chairpersons - CBS AON 2025 is set to raise the bar for school-level diplomatic conferences. We invite schools and aspiring diplomats from across the city and beyond to join us in this celebration of dialogue and diplomacy. So join the legacy of excellence, embrace the rebirth, and make your mark at CBS AON 2025. Mark your calendars for this July and prepare to rise to the occasion at CBS AON 2025 - the ultimate arena for debate, diplomacy, and discovery! Step into the world of international affairs, test your mettle against the best, and be part of a tradition that shapes future leaders.
