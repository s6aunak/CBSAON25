MR. RAJA MCGEE

Principal & Secretary Of Calcutta Boys' School

Calcutta Boys' School presents Assembly of Nations (AON 2025)

It is with immense joy that I warmly invite all delegates, faculty advisors, and esteemed guests to the relaunch of the Calcutta Boys' School - Assembly of Nations (AON 2025) - an event last held in 2019, now returning with renewed zeal, energy, and enthusiasm. AON has never been merely a simulation of international diplomacy. It is a celebration of intellect, a platform for meaningful dialogue, and a crucible for shaping the leaders of tomorrow. In a world that continues to evolve and grapple with complex global challenges, it is more important than ever to nurture young minds that can think critically, speak confidently, and lead with integrity. At Calcutta Boys' School, we take immense pride in offering students opportunities to extend their learning beyond the classroom and engage with the broader world. The Assembly of Nations is our commitment to fostering global citizenship - an understanding that diplomacy is not about domination, but about dialogue; not about division, but about unity and cooperation.

As we bring back AON after a significant hiatus, we do so with the firm belief that this generation is ready - not only to inherit the world, but to shape it for the better. I encourage all delegates to enter the committee rooms with open minds and leave with deeper understanding, stronger convictions, and a renewed sense of purpose.

We look forward to spirited debate, innovative solutions, and lasting friendships at this edition of AON.

Warm regards,
Mr. Raja McGee
Principal and Secretary
