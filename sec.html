OUR SECRETARIAT

Adrish Hossain

Secretary General

Adrish Hossain

Adrish Hossain, the Secretary-General of CBS AON'25, his presence in the MUN circuit looms large, albeit for all the wrong reasons. A national-level debater and a burgeoning MUN enthusiast, he is perpetually on a quest to secure that elusive Best Delegate award every time when he walks in as a delegate, and in that process has earned a remarkable Outstanding Delegate streak ( trust the process ). For anyone who witnessed his debut at an MUN, as the CBS AON secretariat, we must inform you that he’s come a long way from having declared an "invasion" on Russia while representing Germany .Outside of MUNs, Adrish is a science student with a keen interest in legal studies (even though he doesn't know how both work in parallel). He’s also a devoted Bayern Munich fan.

Despite the significant changes since his first MUN in November 2023, two things about him remain unchanged: his love for International Relations and a strong belief that action orders work better than draft resolutions (questionable?). Although he is relatively new to the circuit, we can assure you that he'll bring the same level of dedicated energy and will undoubtedly annoy both his delegates and the Secretariat with his never-ending critiques about "scope for improvements" and "lack of elaboration" in an outrageous 80-page communique.

Favorite Place to be: CBS-AON (he has no life outside of MUNs)

Deputy Secretary General

Priyam Deb

Priyam Deb, Deputy Secretary-General, is the human embodiment of chaos. He chose to become a lawyer solely because he binge-watched Suits and got delusions of Harvey Specter. Priyam Banerjee

USG (Delegate Affairs)

Priyam Banerjee

Priyam Banerjee is someone who thrives in the heat of an argument, whether it’s a courtroom clash in a moot or a high-stakes debate in a Model United Nations committee. Moot courts and MUNs aren’t just hobbies for him — they’re his arena to challenge ideas, stand his ground, and speak for what he believes in. For him, life feels a lot like ranking up in a game — always chasing the next level, with no space for deranking. And when it’s time to vibe, it’s music with soul, something raw and real when words fall short. Priyam is a mix of grit, loyalty, ambition, and a little beautiful chaos.

Antariksh Mondal

USG (Publications)

Antariksh Mondal

Antariksh is the kind of person whose calm demeanor masks a mind constantly at war — with ideas, arguments, and problems waiting to be solved. A national-level debater with a trail of accolades behind him, he doesn’t just speak — he commands the room. His voice doesn’t just argue; it persuades, challenges, and sometimes, even changes minds. Mathematics is his mental gym, and problems are puzzles he refuses to leave unsolved. When the words aren’t spoken, they flow from his pen — sharp, vivid, and immersive, his writing breathes life into characters and worlds that feel strikingly real. Antariksh is the kind of friend everyone wants — kind, grounded, and mature beyond his years. He’s driven by passion, led by purpose, and anchored by loyalty. And when the noise fades, it’s music that fills the silence — soulful, reflective, and just a little bit like him.

Arnab Dash

Arnab Dash, the finance head for MUN, may not be a financial genius (yet), but he’s learning fast and has just enough budgeting sense to help keep things on track—and occasionally even under budget. USG (Tech)

Mahir Khan

Co-Chairperson

Agnitra Saha

Co-Chairperson

Sarbhanu Nath
